How to train?

Create a routine, do not train legs every single day, but do it weekly. Additionally, make sure to exercise all the legs muscles, including glutes, calves, and hamstrings.

What to eat?

Carbs: traditional or sweet potato, quinoa, banana, oats, rice, pasta, vegetables, salmon. Protein: eggs, yogurt, cheese, chicken. Fats: avocado, nuts.

How many times should I train legs?

It varies depending on the person and their objective. However, research says that to gain muscle it is needed a trainning of at least twice a week.
